When Should You Call a Handyman?

How to decide whether a small repair, installation or maintenance job is right for a handyman.

A handyman is often a good fit when a project is too time-consuming for a homeowner but does not require a large remodeling crew.

Good handyman projects

  • Door adjustments and hardware replacement
  • Drywall patching and touch-up painting
  • TV, shelf, mirror and curtain rod mounting
  • Minor carpentry and trim repairs
  • Deck and fence maintenance
  • Small property punch lists

When to call a specialized contractor

Projects involving major structural changes, extensive plumbing or electrical work, roofing, HVAC systems, permits or specialized licenses may require a licensed trade contractor or general contractor.

How to prepare

Take clear photos, list each task, note the property address and explain your ideal timing. This helps the service provider evaluate the visit more efficiently.
