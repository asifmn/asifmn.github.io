Common Drywall Problems and What Causes Them

Learn what causes nail pops, cracks, dents, water stains and damaged corners.

Drywall damage is common in lived-in homes and commercial properties. The repair method depends on the cause, not just the visible mark.

Nail pops

Fasteners can move slightly as framing expands and contracts. A lasting repair usually includes securing the drywall before patching and repainting.

Cracks

Small cracks may result from normal movement. Repeated or widening cracks deserve closer inspection, especially near doors, windows or ceilings.

Dents and holes

Small dents can often be filled and sanded. Larger holes may require a backing piece or replacement section.

Water stains

Do not cover a water stain until the leak or moisture source is identified and corrected. Stain-blocking primer may be needed before repainting.
