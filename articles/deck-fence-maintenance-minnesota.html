Deck and Fence Maintenance in Central Minnesota

Help wood surfaces last longer through seasonal inspection, cleaning and repairs.

Decks and fences in Central Minnesota face moisture, sunlight, snow and freeze-thaw movement. Regular inspection can extend their useful life.

Inspect connections

Look for loose fasteners, moving railings, sagging gates and boards that feel soft or unstable.

Clean carefully

Dirt and organic growth hold moisture against wood. Pressure washing can help, but excessive pressure can damage fibers and create splinters.

Protect exposed wood

Stain or protective coating may reduce moisture absorption. Preparation and dry weather are important for a durable finish.

Repair before winter

Loose boards and railings can become more hazardous when covered by snow or ice.
