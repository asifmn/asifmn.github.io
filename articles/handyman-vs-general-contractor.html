Handyman vs. General Contractor: Which Do You Need?

A simple comparison to help homeowners choose the right professional for a project.

The right choice depends on project size, complexity, licensing and coordination needs.

Choose a handyman for

  • Small repairs and maintenance
  • Mounting and installations
  • Painting touch-ups
  • Minor carpentry
  • Property punch lists

Choose a general contractor for

  • Large remodels
  • Projects involving multiple licensed trades
  • Structural changes
  • Permit-heavy work
  • Complex scheduling and subcontractor management

Ask before scheduling

Describe the work clearly and ask whether the provider is qualified and permitted to perform that exact scope in your location.
